title: 'Getting support'
description: "Support channels, SLA expectations, and escalation guidance for Gameye operators."
---

Use `support@gameye.com` for production-impacting issues and onboarding/escalation requests.

If your issue is non-urgent, you can also use your team’s agreed support channels (for example Slack or Discord).

## Priority and Response Targets

Use one of these priority levels in your subject line:

| Tier | Target response | Example |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Low | Best effort | Feature questions, advisory requests, non-blocking improvements |
| Medium | 2 hours | Partial impact (for example intermittent join failures) |
| High | 1 hour | Major impact (for example most users cannot join) |
| Urgent | 30 minutes | Full outage or critical launch blocker |

## Subject Line Format

Include priority in square brackets so ticket routing is accurate.

Example: `Match sessions unavailable in eu-west [High]`

If no priority is included, requests are treated as low priority by default.

## Escalation Tips

- Include timestamps, affected regions, and session IDs.
- Include request/response snippets and error identifiers where possible.
- State current business impact and expected decision window.
