Because of how we utilize our capacity in our managed solution, we can scale laterally via several locations within the region while also being able to burst into flexible bare metal & cloud solutions once capacity is reached.

The ease of Gameye also makes deploying your game globally so easy, once you push the game server build to the image repository we automatically distribute it across all our regions

Gameye immediately makes this available in all locations. With our ethos on usage, **you only pay for what you use**.
