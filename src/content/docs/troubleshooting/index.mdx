### I can’t see a page in the menu

- Your account may not have access to that section.
- Ask your administrator to grant access if you need it.

### I can’t see an action button (Add/Edit/Delete/Start/Stop)

- Your role may allow viewing but not changing.
- Ask your administrator to grant the required access.

### The page is empty

Common reasons:

- There is no data for your organization/environment.
- Your access is limited to a subset of data.

### I’m getting blocked when opening a page

- You may not have access to that area.
- Contact your administrator with the page name you tried to open.
