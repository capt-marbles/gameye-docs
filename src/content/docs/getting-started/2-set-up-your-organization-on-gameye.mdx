After Docker setup is complete, Gameye configures your organization and image mapping on our side.

## What Gameye Sets Up

- Your organization in the platform.
- Your image/repository registration for orchestration.

## 1. Get Your Authentication Key

We provide a bearer token for your matchmaker and backend integration.

> **Info:** You can validate this key early using tools like Postman or Hoppscotch.

## 2. Test on Sandbox

Validate behavior in sandbox before production rollout:

- Location availability
- Connectivity and latency
- Session startup behavior

Sandbox does not auto-scale like production. If you need temporary capacity, contact support on [Discord](https://bit.ly/3oZZaGR).
