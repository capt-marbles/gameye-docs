### Purpose

Your Profile page lets you view your account information and change your password.

### What you see

**Account information ** panel**Change password** form

![Legacy documentation image](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/_em5a3TH-KfZcBHkdKiWt/Bpi0aET4ehnSggQoSa8Ls_image.png)

### How to: change your password

Open **Profile**.

Enter your current password.

Enter and confirm your new password.

Submit.
