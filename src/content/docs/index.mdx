Gameye is the orchestrator for demanding games. It lets you easily host your multiplayer games, orchestrating sessions across multiple providers and locations using high-performing servers. With it, you get the capacity to meet sudden demand, such as a surge of new players. We’re on a mission to host the world’s most demanding games flawlessly. Your players will not only have a **place to play,** but the best place to play. Always.

## What do you get?

- Manage servers automatically
- Monitor your sessions in real-time through our API.
- Scale across regions seamlessly with no studio intervention
- Upload once, deploy everywhere
- Zero downtime.
- Agnostic by nature: multiple server providers, integrate with whatever matchmaker or other tools you have.
- A billing structure that means you only pay for what you use.
- Cut down on queue times by using**Smarthost (BETA) **to predict demand.
- 24/7 support, real people, no robots!

## Scaling your sessions

We can automatically increase and decrease how many servers you're using because we have providers from all over the world and a mix of cloud, bare-metal and flex-metal.

>**INFO:** **Strive for redundancy**. It's our policy to always have redundancy in each of our regions. That way, if one provider goes down (or has problems), we can switch to an alternative.
