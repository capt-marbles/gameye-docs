# Purpose

Gameye Admin helps you view and manage the operational resources that power your applications (applications, containers, nodes, images, and supporting configuration such as regions and port bindings).

This manual is written for **non-system roles** (not SUPER\_ADMIN). Some sections or actions may not be available to you depending on your access.

## How access affects what you see

- Some menu items may be hidden.
- Some buttons (for example**Add**,**Edit**,**Delete**,**Start**,**Stop**) may be hidden.
- If you can open a page but cannot perform an action, you may see the data but not the action controls.

If something described here is not visible in your account, contact your administrator.
