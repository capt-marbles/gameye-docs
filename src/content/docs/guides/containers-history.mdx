### Purpose

Containers History is used to review previously started containers and historical activity.

### What you see

A table of historical container entries. Exact columns depend on your environment.

![Legacy documentation image](https://archbee-image-uploads.s3.amazonaws.com/_em5a3TH-KfZcBHkdKiWt/S20GnytJnWhTrh2MYXopq_image.png)

### Common actions

View-only.

Search, sort, and filter are available.
